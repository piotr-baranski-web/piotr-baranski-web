About me

I'm a graphic designer, motion designer, and UI/UX designer with strong technical background and experience working with marketing teams. In my projects, I combine design, animation, and technology to create comprehensive visual solutions for brands and digital products.

On a daily basis, I work with Adobe Creative Cloud, Figma, Miro, Hotjar, and Google Analytics tools that support me in researching user behavior, designing interfaces and optimizing experiences. I implement my projects in CMS systems. Additionally, I use AI technologies such as ChatGPT, Midjourney, Cursor, and Bolt to accelerate creative processes, from prototyping to generating visual concepts, coding, copy optimization, and automating repetitive tasks.

I create visual identities, website and application layouts, motion animations, banners, campaign creatives, and digital and social media materials. I design based on UX principles and the latest UI trends, ensuring consistency, aesthetics, and effectiveness of visual communication.
